About

We opened with one rule.

Tide & Larder opened in 2019, a few blocks up from the harbor. The rule hasn't changed: nothing goes on the menu older than the tide it came in on.

We work with four day boats between Casco Bay and Georges Bank, and the raw bar changes depending on what they radio in before service. Portions are sized for the table, not the plate — most mains are meant to be shared down the middle.

Sourcing
Four boats, one phone call each morning. If they didn't catch it, it's not on the board.
Prep
Whole fish are broken down in-house. Bones go into stock the same day.
Service
Tables are timed loosely. We'd rather you stay too long than feel rushed.
